# Human-centered AI should make work feel more personal, not less

The best use of AI is not simply to automate people out of the conversation. It is to help people think more clearly, respond more thoughtfully, and serve others with better context.

In business and finance, that distinction matters. A tool can be fast and still feel cold. A workflow can be efficient and still miss the human need underneath the request.

I am interested in AI systems that improve judgment, preparation, and communication while keeping the person responsible for the relationship. That is where AI becomes less of a replacement story and more of a leverage story.

> The question I keep coming back to: does this technology make the human interaction better?

That is the kind of problem I want to keep exploring here.
