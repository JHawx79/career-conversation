## Lean Purpose-Built AI Solutions Beat Bloated Platforms

The more AI software that floods the market, the more I'm seeing lean, purpose-built solutions leaving bloated platforms in the dust.

Everyone is racing to build the next AI everything tool. 50 features, 200 integrations, a dashboard that looks like a cockpit.

But in business, the real advantage isn't total features — it's your process.

Fundamentals haven't changed. Good process design still has the edge over a fancy tool with no process or a bad process behind it.

What I'm seeing now is that AI amplifies whatever you already have. If your procedural through line is messy, AI just makes the mess bigger and faster. If your concept, workflow, or tasks are tight, AI makes them scalable.

So before you buy the next AI tool, ask: what is my process, and how does it leverage this?

Because right now, the smartest move isn't adding more AI — it's designing better fundamentals.
